Our Story

McShane Roofing is a family-run, independent roofing service based in Hastings & St.Leonards-On-Sea. Business owner and roofer Jake McShane has over 10 years experience in the roofing industry. He decided to set up McShane Roofing in 2015, with the aim of providing a higher quality and more affordable service than what was currently on offer in Hastings.

McShane Roofing is now well established in Hastings & St.Leonards, and we have been able to expand our service to much of the Sout East. We offer quality & reliable roofing fixes at affordable prices; including roofing repairs, roof replacements, and maintenance.
