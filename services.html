Lead Work

Did you know that lead work on your roof is equally as important as the roof covering itself? From lead roofs and step flashing, to back gutters and decorative bays, we make sure your lead is correctly installed, dressed and pointed, ensuring a long-lasting and durable finish.

Commercial Roofs

From leaking guttering, to damaged asbestos sheeting or broken fixings, commercial roofs can be a pain to fix. We understand the need for business to continue trading as normal during improvement works. We have the right access equipment required to work on all commercial roofs. And our certified access equipment safely lets us work on your asbestos sheeting.

Chimney Flashings

If your pointing has failed or your chimney is leaking; we carry out re-pointing, new lead flashings, new render and pot installation and flaunching. We can also fit bird spikes and chimney cowls to stop rain and birds getting into your chimney.

Tiling

Is your roof leaking or damaged? We carry out small repairs to complete stripping and re-tiling your roof. Our replacement service is tailored to your roofing needs; with custom tiles, ventilation and dry systems available as part of the process.

Slating

If you're looking for an elegant and lost-lasting roof replacement, our team provides an excellent services for all natural and manmade slates. Unlike alot of contractors, our team provides excellent installation for slate roofs.

Flat Roofs

Whether you have a small flat roof to a big flat roof, we have many different repair and renewal options available. From fibreglass to liquid rubber, insulated and vented roofs - no job is too big or small!

Rendering & Guttering

McShane Roofing can provide a wide variety of services including cleaning and maintaining your existing guttering or new installations. We are happy to discuss available options with you and can provide quotations as requested, including firewalls, chimneys, and rainwater outlets.

Waterproofing

Hot melt, single ply, liquid waterproofing products. Our waterproofing solutions are sure to prevent roof leaks and significantly increase the lifespan of your roof, new or old. One of the core strengths is the ability to tailor our structure to our clients business. So our solutions are built around your building.

Small Projects

Not every project requires a full roofing team. If you have a detached garage, shed, or conservatory project, we have one of our expert roofers standing by to help.
